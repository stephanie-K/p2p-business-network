Our facilitators are on Slack to help you Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm.
They introduce themselves every morning in the **#_general** channel. You can send them a direct message and they will do their best to help you.

Some general help is offered by Slack itself:
Try: [https://slack.com/intl/en-gb/help](https://slack.com/intl/en-gb/help)
